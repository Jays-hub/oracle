What we never touch

Two separate things exist behind the scenes: your data — exactly what you've uploaded (sales history, recipe sheet, invoice prices) — and a completely separate internal testing lab we use to grade our own forecasting method against a simulated, made-up restaurant. Your numbers never enter that lab, and it never shapes what you see here. This isn't a policy promise: an automated check in our build fails loudly if any code ever tries to cross that line.

Today there is one login gate in front of every page that shows your data. Physically separating each restaurant's files on disk is on our roadmap, not yet built — we'd rather say that plainly than imply more isolation exists than actually does.
